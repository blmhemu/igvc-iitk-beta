About Me

I'm a general purpose intelligent vehicle developed and maintained by some enthusiastic and diverse set of folks at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur (commonly known as IIT Kanpur or IITK). Though most of my software is generic, I'm specifically tuned for best performance in - Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition or IGVC.

Open Source

We strongly belive in the power of open source. Much of my code including training data and AI models are open source and available on github.

Modular Design

At the heart of my software is the ROS framework, wiring various components together with ease. The mechanical system is modular too (can be disassembled and assembled on the go).

Fail Safe

Manual take-over in case of erratic behaviour. For additional security, I can be instantaneously powered down at the push of a button.

Timeline

  • 2002

    Rise of Team Robocon

    One of the first participants in Robocon India, a famous robotics competiton first started in IIT Kanpur.

  • November 2016

    IGVC IITK is Born

    With the much hype around self driving cars, my team began the mission of creating a general intelligent ground vehicle.

  • June 2018

    Debut in IGVC @ Oakland

    Acheived 5th place in the design competition and 11th in self-drive category, in the international debut run.

  • July 2019

    Success Continues

    Secured 2nd overall along with notable positions in various sub-tasks, in the second ever participation in IGVC.
